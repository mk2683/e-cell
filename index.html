About Us


Entrepreneurship Cell GGSIPU, a non-profit organization that aims to bring together the entrepreneurial passions to converge on a common platform. Since from existence in 2014, E-Cell GGSIPU has aimed to bring to the forth the entrepreneur dwelling within so many of us. To accomplish the goal of serving as a platform for networking and interaction, E-Cell GGSIPU has been organising a host of events.
Enterpreneurship is for improving the lives of others and for leaving your community and world better than you found it.
